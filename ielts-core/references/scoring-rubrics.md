# IELTS Scoring Rubrics For Coaching

Use this as a coaching rubric, not as an official scoring replacement. For current official descriptors, check the IELTS links in `current-ielts-facts.md`.

## Writing

Criteria:

- Task Achievement / Task Response
- Coherence and Cohesion
- Lexical Resource
- Grammatical Range and Accuracy

Practical band checks:

| Band | Coaching signal |
|------|-----------------|
| 8 | Fully addresses the task, clear progression, flexible vocabulary, rare errors |
| 7 | Addresses all parts, clear position, logical paragraphs, some less common vocabulary, errors do not impede communication |
| 6 | Addresses the task but development may be thin; cohesion may be mechanical; vocabulary is adequate but imprecise; grammar errors are noticeable |
| 5 | Partial response, limited development, weak paragraphing, frequent lexical/grammar problems |

When scoring:

1. Quote evidence from the user's text.
2. Give a criterion score or range.
3. Explain the highest-impact fix.
4. State uncertainty when the evidence is thin.

## Speaking

Criteria:

- Fluency and Coherence
- Lexical Resource
- Grammatical Range and Accuracy
- Pronunciation

Practical band checks:

| Band | Coaching signal |
|------|-----------------|
| 8 | Fluent with only occasional hesitation, flexible language, mostly error-free complex structures, pronunciation is easy to follow |
| 7 | Speaks at length, some hesitation or self-correction, enough topic vocabulary, varied structures with some errors |
| 6 | Maintains speech but with pauses/repetition, vocabulary is adequate, grammar range is mixed, pronunciation generally intelligible |
| 5 | Frequent pauses, limited vocabulary, limited grammar range, pronunciation may strain the listener |

## Listening And Reading

- Each test has 40 questions.
- Each correct answer earns 1 raw mark.
- Raw score to band conversion can vary slightly by test version.
- Use conversion tables as practice estimates only.

## Overall Score

Average the four section scores, then round to the nearest whole or half band.

- .25 rounds up to the next half band.
- .75 rounds up to the next whole band.
