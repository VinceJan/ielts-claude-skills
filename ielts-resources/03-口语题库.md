## Part 1 四大必考话题

每次考试 Part 1 必从以下四个话题开始：

1. **Home**（公寓/房子/村子/城市）
2. **Hometown**（城市/乡村/地方特色）
3. **Work**（工作内容/喜好/同事）
4. **Study**（专业/课程/学校）

## Part 2 话题卡（2026 年 1-8 月）

### 人物类

1. Describe someone who is good at their job
2. Describe a friend from your childhood
3. Describe a person who inspired you
4. Describe a family member you spend the most time with
5. Describe someone you would like to meet
6. Describe an old person you respect

### 地点类

7. Describe a quiet place you like to go
8. Describe a city you have visited
9. Describe a place you would like to visit in the future
10. Describe your favourite room in your home
11. Describe a shopping mall you often go to

### 经历类

12. Describe a memorable journey you have had
13. Describe a time when you felt proud of a family member
14. Describe a challenge you overcame
15. Describe a time you received good news
16. Describe an occasion when you were late
17. Describe a time you helped someone
18. Describe a perfect job you would like to have in the future
19. Describe a time you changed your mind about something

### 物品类

20. Describe a gift you gave to someone
21. Describe a piece of technology (not a phone) you would like to own
22. Describe a book you have read recently
23. Describe a story (fairy tale, etc.) you have read recently
24. Describe a program or app on your computer or phone
25. Describe something you own that is important to you

### 活动类

26. Describe a sport or exercise you enjoy
27. Describe a skill you would like to learn
28. Describe a hobby you started recently
29. Describe a bicycle/motorcycle/car trip
30. Describe a festival or celebration in your country
31. Describe an outdoor activity you enjoy
32. Describe a time you drew a picture or took photos

## Part 3 讨论主题

### 科技与社会

- How has technology changed the way people learn?
- Is artificial intelligence more opportunity or risk for the workforce?
- What are the ethics of data collection and privacy?
- How has social media affected interpersonal relationships?

### 环境与可持续

- Should individuals or governments take more responsibility for the environment?
- What is the economic argument for or against decarbonisation?
- Do people in developed nations have greater responsibility to reduce consumption?

### 教育

- Does formal education prepare young people for the modern workplace?
- Is university education still worth the rising tuition costs?
- How has the role of teachers changed with online learning?

### 健康与幸福

- What is the relationship between modern work culture and mental health?
- Should governments do more to promote healthy lifestyles?
- Is happiness something that can be learned?

### 工作与职业

- What skills will be most important for future jobs?
- Is work-life balance achievable in today's economy?
- Should people choose jobs based on salary or passion?
