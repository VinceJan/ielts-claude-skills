## Task 2 高频话题分类

### 教育 Education

- Is university education worth the cost?
- Should children start learning foreign languages at primary school?
- Is online learning as effective as classroom learning?
- Should schools teach practical skills alongside academic subjects?
- Is competition in education beneficial or harmful?

### 科技 Technology

- Has technology made our lives better or worse?
- Should AI be used in education?
- Is social media doing more harm than good?
- Will robots replace human workers?
- Should governments regulate the internet?

### 环境 Environment

- Should individuals or governments take responsibility for environmental protection?
- Is economic growth compatible with environmental protection?
- Should plastic bags be banned?
- Is nuclear energy the solution to climate change?
- Should developing countries sacrifice economic growth for environmental protection?

### 健康 Health

- Should governments do more to promote healthy lifestyles?
- Is fast food the main cause of obesity?
- Should healthcare be free for everyone?
- Is mental health as important as physical health?
- Should junk food advertising be banned?

### 社会 Society

- Is the gap between rich and growing?
- Should the retirement age be raised?
- Is city life better than rural life?
- Should governments control population growth?
- Is globalization beneficial for all countries?

### 工作 Work

- Is work-life balance achievable?
- Should people choose jobs based on salary or passion?
- Is working from home better than working in an office?
- Should women have equal opportunities in the workplace?
- Is job satisfaction more important than job security?

### 犯罪 Crime

- Should criminals be punished or rehabilitated?
- Is the death penalty an effective deterrent?
- Should juvenile offenders be treated differently?
- Is prison the best solution for all crimes?
- Should drug use be decriminalized?

### 媒体 Media

- Is the news media trustworthy?
- Should there be censorship of the internet?
- Are newspapers still relevant in the digital age?
- Is advertising harmful to society?
- Should children be allowed to use social media?

## Task 2 题型分布

| 题型 | 出现频率 | 关键要求 |
|------|---------|---------|
| Opinion (Agree/Disagree) | ~40% | 明确立场，贯穿全文 |
| Discussion (Both views) | ~25% | 讨论双方 + 给出自己观点 |
| Advantages/Disadvantages | ~15% | 分析利弊 + 可能需要给观点 |
| Problem/Solution | ~10% | 分析问题 + 提出解决方案 |
| Two-part question | ~10% | 回答两个问题 |

## Task 1 图表类型分布（Academic）

| 图表类型 | 出现频率 | 关键要求 |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 柱状图 Bar Chart | ~30% | 比较数据差异 |
| 折线图 Line Graph | ~25% | 描述趋势变化 |
| 饼图 Pie Chart | ~15% | 描述比例关系 |
| 表格 Table | ~15% | 提取关键数据 |
| 地图 Map | ~8% | 对比两个地点/时期 |
| 流程图 Process | ~7% | 描述步骤顺序 |

## 高分表达积累

### 引出观点

- It is widely believed that...
- There is a growing body of evidence suggesting...
- It is often argued that...
- Many people hold the view that...

### 表达立场

- I strongly believe that...
- In my opinion...
- From my perspective...
- I am convinced that...

### 举例

- For instance,...
- A good example of this is...
- To illustrate this point,...
- This is evidenced by...

### 对比

- However,...
- On the other hand,...
- In contrast,...
- Nevertheless,...

### 让步

- While it is true that..., I still believe...
- Admittedly,... however,...
- Despite the fact that..., I maintain that...

### 总结

- In conclusion,...
- To sum up,...
- All things considered,...
- Taking everything into account,...
